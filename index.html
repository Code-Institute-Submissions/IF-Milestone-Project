How to play Blackjack

At the start of a hand, you are dealt two cards, and the dealer is dealt one. You can choose to 'Hit' (draw another card); or 'Stand' (accept the hand you currently have).

The Main goal is to get a higher value hand than the dealer, without going over 21.

If you go over 21, you lose, regardless of the dealer's hand.

If you are dealt an Ace and any 10-value card (10, jack, queen, or king) you have a 'blackjack' and automatically win that deal.

The dealer will draw until hitting 17.
